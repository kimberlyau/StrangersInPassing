Tips and Tricks for Traveling Around (Southern) California on a Budget

Method of Transportation Key Notes
1. Train (Amtrak or Metrolink) Although Amtrak offers more convenient locations, Metrolink can prove to cost less if you are making a day trip on the weekends as it offers a $10 day pass.
Students: Look into student discounts and plan your trip early!!!
2. Rideshare Oftentimes university students have rideshare pages where students give each other rides. Generally safe as most pages are closed to only students of a particular university.
However still use with caution
